On the dashboard

Everything that decides your day on the water

One pull of every live feed, read at a glance — then out on the water when you need it.

At-a-glance rating

A Safe / Caution / Dangerous boating read, recomputed on every refresh from wind, waves, storms, and water.

Wind & waves

Sustained wind, gusts, and a wave-height estimate tuned to each lake's shape and the direction the wind is blowing from.

Water levels

Live pool and tailwater elevation versus summer pool, with a chart of the last three days and a five-day forecast.

Live radar

Animated NEXRAD radar centered on your lake with your own position shown, so you can read a cell's track before it arrives.

Man-overboard

Mark a spot instantly, then get live bearing, distance, and coordinates to relay. The mark survives an app relaunch.

Works with no signal

Last readings are cached per lake and clearly labeled when offline. Emergency checklists and a Live Activity keep working in the dead zones.
